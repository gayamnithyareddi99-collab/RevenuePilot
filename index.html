Core Innovation Modules

Five Proprietary Engines. One Governance OS.

Engine 01

Behavior-to-Outcome Governance Graph

A first-of-its-kind framework that bridges the gap between live conversational signals and backend commercial policy.

Captures hidden negotiation patterns and SIMOPs conflicts, storing them as structured benchmarked commercial objects. The graph detects "Deal DNA" including the impact of unaddressed objections, micro-shifts in buyer sentiment, and discount-induced urgency enabling personalized "Policy-to-Prompt" pathways instantly.
Engine 02

Revenue-Quality Ontology (RQO)

A proprietary schema using ML to identify and link detected conversational entities to precise deal-health coordinates.

The RQO converts raw call data into structured "Behavioral Objects," ensuring that the strategic nuances of specific sales methodologies (MEDDIC, Challenger, Founder-led) are preserved. Enables rapid benchmarking and cloning of optimal closing logic across the team turning transient "street knowledge" into permanent Governance Equity.
Engine 03

Negotiation Delta Diagnostics (NDD)

Automatically diagnoses root causes of margin erosion by analyzing the delta between expected policy and live rep behavior.

The NDD mechanism identifies early-stage discounting, missed suitability questions, and unauthorized concessions in real time. When a deal risk profile shifts, the platform deconstructs the change into a structured "Commercial Interference Report" without requiring manual manager review significantly reducing deal-review time.
Engine 04

Policy-Inference Autocalibration (PIAC)

An AI-driven process that continuously self-calibrates governance thresholds from diverse data sources.

PIAC triangulates data from Zoom/Teams audio, CRM history, and internal pricing rules eliminating the need for manual script re-writing. The system learns from historical deal interaction trends and outcome data (defaults, refunds) to improve Policy-to-Prompt pathways automatically over time.
Engine 05

Commercial Stewardship Index (CSI)

A composite measure assessing the reliability of automated sales workflows reducing 20%+ margin leakage at scale.

The CSI converts daily sales interactions into proprietary institutional assets. While competitors focus on "Closed-Won" speed, we identify that a heavy discount satisfying a short-term revenue goal may result in a drop in long-term profitability via qualification-drift and unmanaged post-sale costs. The CSI generates leadership-ready audit reports aligned with UK FCA Consumer Duty 2025/26.
Engine 06

Community Revenue Intelligence Network

Federated learning architecture enabling anonymised "Revenue-Quality DNA" to improve predictive models sector-wide.

Anonymised benchmarks from dozens of UK independent firms constantly improve predictive models without sharing sensitive commercial data. Trends from high-efficiency advisory firms in London can be used to optimise governance strategy for a new consultative business in Manchester providing independent firms with strategic intelligence usually reserved for global consulting giants.
